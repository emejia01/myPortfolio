About Erik


I am currently an undergrad student at Manhattan College in New York City. I was born and raised in Yonkers, NY, a neighboring city to the Bronx. Although New York is where I was raised, I’ve always seen myself traveling throughout the country to experience new places!


During my time at Manhattan College I was always interested in Engineering, however it wasn’t until after my freshman year that I considered majoring in Computer Science. In fact, I was originally a Mechanical Engineering major before I was introduced to programming. However, when I finally decided to make the ‘swap’ to Computer Science I had come to find out that I was one of the best decisions I’d made in my life!


Currently I am in the middle of completing my undergrad degree in CS with a concentration in Machine Learning and Intelligence and a Mathematics minor. Some of my relevant course work includes Data Structures and Algorithms, Computer Security, Data Mining, Object Oriented Design with Java and Linear Algebra and Statistics. Aside from school work, I spend a lot of my time working on personal projects. One of my favorites was a Class Registration Bot for students at my University. Watching students use it for their own schedules was especially rewarding.




Personal Projects


News Scraper and Sentiment Analyzer

This is a console-based Python program that is able to extract the textual data from the URL of a news article webpage (Currently works with CNN, MSNBC/NBC News, The Washington Post, and Fox News ). After extracting the data, the text is then analyzed against a list of positive and negative sentiment words to determine whether the narrative of the article is positive or negative.






Manhattan-College-Class-Registration-Automator

AutoReg is a python GUI based program that allows Manhattan College students to register for their desired courses as soon as the registration portal time opens. This program automates the registration process so that actual registration time happens in milliseconds, increasing the probability of successfully registering for all your desired classes.










Auto-Mate Registration Tool: Chrome Extension

COMMING SOON! Auto-Mate.io is a Chrome Browser Extension and is a continuation of my AutoReg project. This extension will provide an easy access tool for Manhattan College students to automate the class registration process. With Auto-mate, students would only need to provide their Manhattan College student credentials and the applicable Course Registration Number of that the program and attempt registration.
